üéØ Core Strategy

My approach revolves around high-time-frame Fair Value Gaps (FVGs) leading to structured continuation models.

Risk management is the cornerstone of my strategy. I risk 0.5‚Äì1% per trade, journal every session, and constantly refine my process through data and psychology review.

  1. Determine market bias (bullish/bearish) using 1D, 4H, and 1H charts.
  2. After determining bias, look for retracement at the equilibrium of the range overlapping a 15m+ FVG.
  3. Once the FVG rebalances, look for an IFVG in the direction of the bias.
  4. Target internal liquidity (HTF FVGs) or external liquidity (major highs/lows).
  5. Aim for 1+ risk-to-reward ratio.
