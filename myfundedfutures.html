💼 Account Types

Learn about evaluation, express, and live-funded accounts — their rules, profit targets, and drawdown limits.

  • Evaluation: Simulated stage to prove consistency.
  • Express: Straightforward one-step evaluation with higher targets.
  • Funded: Real trading stage with daily/overall drawdown rules.

📏 Rules & Objectives

  • Meet profit target within the trading period.
  • Avoid daily or max drawdown breaches.
  • Trade minimum required days before passing.
  • Use approved platforms (Tradovate, Rithmic, etc.).

💻 Platform Setup

How to connect your evaluation account to a platform:

  1. Purchase your challenge on MyFundedFutures.com.
  2. Wait for credentials email from Rithmic or Tradovate.
  3. Install R|Trader Pro or Tradovate.
  4. Connect using your evaluation login and begin paper trading.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is MyFundedFutures legit? Yes, it’s a registered prop firm offering simulated and live trading opportunities.
  • What markets can I trade? CME futures — ES, NQ, CL, GC, etc.
  • How long does funding take? Usually 1–2 business days after passing the evaluation.
  • Is overnight trading allowed? Yes, depending on account type.

🧩 Troubleshooting

If you face login or connection issues:

  • Ensure correct username and server (e.g., Rithmic Paper Trading).
  • Restart the platform after credential changes.
  • Contact support@myfundedfutures.com.
