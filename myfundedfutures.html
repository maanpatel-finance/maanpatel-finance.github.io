💼 Account Types

Understand each account type before starting your challenge:

  • Evaluation: Simulated stage to demonstrate consistency and risk control.
  • Sim Funded: Achieved after passing eval, work your way to payouts.
  • Live Funded: Granted after consistent performance on Sim, trading with live capital rather than sim.

📏 Rules & Objectives

  • Reach your profit target within the trading period.
  • Avoid daily and total drawdown violations.
  • Trade minimum required days to prove consistency.
  • Use only approved trading platforms (Tradovate, Rithmic, etc.).

💻 Platform Setup

Follow these steps to connect your account to a trading platform:

  1. Purchase your evaluation on MyFundedFutures.com.
  2. Wait for your Rithmic or Tradovate credentials via email.
  3. Install R|Trader Pro or Tradovate.
  4. Connect and begin paper trading to meet consistency metrics.

🎯 Trading Strategy

Apply a systematic, rule-based strategy focusing on bias, structure, and precision.

  1. Determine directional bias using higher time frames.
  2. Wait for retracement into a Fair Value Gap (FVG).
  3. Use intraday confirmation (IFVGs, liquidity sweeps).
  4. Set risk at 0.5–1% per trade and target 1–2R returns.

🧠 Practice Plan

Develop consistency through replay sessions, journaling, and self-review using tools like Tradovate Replay or NinjaTrader Market Replay.
