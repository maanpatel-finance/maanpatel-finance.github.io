1. Introduction — What is MyFundedFutures?

MyFundedFutures is a futures prop trading firm offering sim-funded evaluations and live-funded accounts. This tutorial walks you from signup to first payout with links to official resources, video walkthroughs, and an actionable practice plan.

Quick roadmap

  1. Choose an account type and evaluate (Challenge)
  2. Pass the simulation step and verify
  3. Receive funded account and follow live rules
  4. Request payouts per payout rules

(Links to MyFundedFutures official site, help center and video channels are in the Resources section.)

2. Account Types & Plans

MyFundedFutures offers multiple plans (Starter, Core/Scale, Pro, Expert) with different objectives, profit split and payout cadence. Choose based on bankroll, risk appetite and target instrument size.

How to choose

  • Starter: good for newer traders or smaller contract sizes.
  • Core / Scale: better for building a scaling plan and accessing larger funded accounts.
  • Pro / Expert: for experienced traders seeking higher caps and different verification rules.

See official plan details in the Resources panel (official site & help docs).

3. Rules, Limits & Risk Management

Rules change — always confirm the latest rules before you trade. Key things to understand:

  • Daily profit targets and minimum withdrawal requirements
  • Trailing loss and session/overnight loss limits
  • Prohibited conduct (e.g., wash trading, sharing accounts)
  • Scaling plan requirements and number of allowed accounts

We link to the official rules and the scaled plan help articles in Resources.

Checklist

  4. Test order sending in a sim environment

5. Simple strategy ideas to practice

Start with simple, repeatable setups you can execute with discipline:

  • Pullback with trend confirmation on 5/15-minute charts
  • Breakout with defined risk (stop below/above breakout candle)
  • Trend-following with ATR-based stops
  • Scalping small moves with strict risk per trade

Document every trade in a trading journal — the single highest-leverage habit for improving fast.

6. 30-Day Practice Plan

Week 1 — Setup & small live-sim trades. Focus: platform familiarity and journal format.

Week 2 — Execute repeatable setups, limit contract size. Focus: entry/exit consistency.

Week 3 — Increase tempo (if consistent) and test scaling rules. Focus: streaks and drawdown handling.

Week 4 — Mimic evaluation rules for consecutive days/daily profit targets.

Daily checklist

  • Review economic calendar
  • Define instrument, bias, and risk per trade
  • Log trades and post-mortem each session

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the evaluation take?

A: Evaluation duration depends on your trading speed and whether you meet objective targets; some traders pass in a few days, others take longer.

Q: Are there limits on accounts?

A: MyFundedFutures has limits (for example: number of sim-funded accounts per household). See the official help center for exact current limits.
