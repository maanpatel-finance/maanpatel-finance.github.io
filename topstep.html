A complete walkthrough of how to join the Topstep futures program, pass the evaluation, and access live funding — by Maan Patel.

🌐 Visit Topstep.com

💼 Account Sizes & Subscription

Choose the size that fits your goals: $50K, $100K, $150K buying power accounts with different profit targets and monthly costs.

  • $50K Buying Power: $49/month, profit target ~$3,000.
  • $100K Buying Power: $99/month, profit target ~$6,000.
  • $150K Buying Power: $149/month, profit target ~$9,000.

📏 Rules & Objectives

  • Meet the profit target to pass the evaluation (varies by account size).
  • Do not exceed the Maximum Loss Limit or your evaluation resets.
  • Follow consistency and daily loss limits to remain eligible.

💻 Platform & Trading Setup

Use TopstepX™ or supported futures trading platforms for your evaluation and live trading experience.

  1. Subscribe to your Combine plan at Topstep.com.
  2. Receive credentials and configure your trading platform.
  3. Connect data feeds and begin simulated trading under evaluation rules.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Topstep? A futures prop firm that funds traders after they pass the evaluation.
  • What can I trade? Futures contracts including E-mini S&P, NASDAQ, Crude Oil, and Gold.
  • How do payouts work? Funded traders can withdraw profits following Topstep’s payout schedule.

🧩 Troubleshooting

Having technical issues? Verify login credentials, check data feed connectivity, and ensure trading hours compliance.

  • Confirm you’re on the correct server.
  • Check that futures data feed symbols are active.
  • Contact Topstep support for persistent issues.
